Typography

Frutiger is the NHS brand font. Email the standards team at service-manual.net for the desktop font.

Body paragraph size is 19px as the baseline.For information dense components and patterns, such as tables, use 16px. Line height should always be 1.5 times the size of the font.

Fallback font

Fallback to Arial when Frutiger isn't available.
